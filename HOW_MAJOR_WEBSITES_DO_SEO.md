## 📊 Common SEO Patterns Across All Major Sites

### 1. **Keyword Placement**
```
✅ URL: /recipes-in-telugu
✅ Title (H1): Recipes in Telugu - Healthy Indian Cooking
✅ First Paragraph: "Looking for recipes in Telugu? Here are..."
✅ H2 Headings: "Breakfast Recipes in Telugu"
✅ Throughout Content: Natural mentions 5-10 times
✅ Image Alt Text: "healthy-breakfast-recipe-in-telugu"
✅ Meta Description: "Find 100+ recipes in Telugu..."
```

### 2. **Content Structure**
```
H1: Main Keyword (once)
  ├─ H2: Keyword Variation 1
  │   ├─ Content with keywords
  │   └─ Internal links
  ├─ H2: Keyword Variation 2
  │   ├─ Content with keywords
  │   └─ Internal links
  └─ H2: Related Topics
      └─ "People Also Search For" keywords
```

### 3. **Internal Linking**
```
Every page links to:
- 3-5 related articles
- Main category page
- Homepage
- "You might also like" section

Anchor text includes keywords:
❌ Bad: "Click here"
✅ Good: "Read our weight loss tips in Telugu"
```

### 4. **Content Length**
```
Homepage: 500-1000 words
Category Pages: 1000-1500 words
Blog Posts: 1500-3000 words
Pillar Content: 3000-5000 words

More content = More keywords = Better SEO
```

## 🔍 How to Find "People Also Search For" Keywords

### Method 1: Google Search (Free)
1. Go to Google.com
2. Search: "recipes in Telugu"
3. Scroll to bottom
4. See "Related searches" or "People also search for"
5. Copy those exact phrases
6. Add them to your About page

### Method 2: Google Search Console (Free)
1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Performance"
3. See "Queries" - these are what people searched
4. Add top queries to your pages

### Method 3: Google Autocomplete (Free)
1. Type in Google: "recipes in telugu"
2. Google suggests:
   - recipes in telugu for breakfast
   - recipes in telugu for lunch
   - recipes in telugu for dinner
   - recipes in telugu for weight loss
3. Add these to your content

### Method 4: "People Also Ask" (Free)
1. Search your keyword
2. See "People also ask" boxes
3. Click to expand questions
4. Use these as H2 headings in your content

## 💡 Pro Tips from Major Websites

### 1. **Answer Questions**
```
❌ Bad Title: "Weight Loss"
✅ Good Title: "How to Lose Weight Fast? 10 Proven Tips"

People search questions:
- "How to lose weight in Telugu?"
- "What to eat for diabetes?"
- "Which diet is best for thyroid?"
```

### 2. **Use Numbers**
```
❌ Bad: "Weight Loss Tips"
✅ Good: "10 Weight Loss Tips That Actually Work"

Numbers get more clicks:
- "5 Best Recipes..."
- "7 Ways to..."
- "10 Tips for..."
```

### 3. **Add Year**
```
❌ Bad: "Diet Plans"
✅ Good: "Best Diet Plans 2026"

Google loves fresh content!
```

### 4. **Use "Best", "Top", "Guide"**
```
- "Best Recipes in Telugu"
- "Top 10 Weight Loss Tips"
- "Complete Guide to Diabetes Diet"
- "Ultimate List of Millet Recipes"
```

### 5. **Create Comparison Content**
```
- "Ragi vs Wheat: Which is Better?"
- "Diabetes Diet vs Normal Diet"
- "Free Diet Plans vs Premium Plans"
```
